Aerospace and Aviation

Aerospace Materials Specialist

To be eligible, candidates must have passed the 10+2 board exam with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM), or hold an AICTE-approved 3-year engineering diploma in any stream with a minimum aggregate of 45% marks.

  • The candidate's age at the time of admission must fall between 16 and 28 years.
  • Candidates must not have any physical disabilities or color blindness that could potentially affect their work.
  • Candidates must pass the AME CET (Common Entrance Test) examination to qualify for the program.
  • Those who are eligible for a B.Tech degree in any stream may also pursue AME courses.
